About Me

I am a mathematician from Nayarit with research interests in commutative algebra and algebraic geometry. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Instituto de Matemáticas, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM). My research focuses on higher-order differential modules, Hasse–Schmidt derivations, jet schemes and arc spaces, and higher Nash blowups. Beyond mathematics, I enjoy music and dancing.

Research Interests

My research interests include commutative algebra, singularity theory. In particular Hasse–Schmidt derivations, higher-order differential modules, jet schemes and arc spaces, Nash and higher Nash blowups, and blow-up modules.
